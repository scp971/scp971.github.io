Generative Artificial Intelligence (GenAI) in Additive Manufacturing (AM)

Goal

Exploration of GenAI and foundation models in AM. This includes building AI models for AM and addressing key challenges such as design optimization, process monitoring, and defect detection.

Overview

Foundation models and Generative AI (GenAI) are rapidly evolving fields.

Foundation Models are large pre-trained deep learning models trained on vast datasets using self-supervision.They Can be fine-tuned for various downstream tasks, including:Text generation (e.g., GPT-3), Image generation (e.g., DALL-E) and Classification and language understanding (e.g., BERT) etc.

Generative AI (GenAI) Models are subsets of foundation models focused on content creation. They are used for creative and generative tasks. They create new content such as:Text (e.g., ChatGPT), Images (e.g., Stable Diffusion),Videos (e.g., Runway Gen-2) etc.

GenAI has gained attention for its potential in various domains, including Additive Manufacturing (AM). This website serves as a resource for understanding how GenAI and foundation models can be leveraged to improve AM processes. It focuses on:

    1. Building and exploring foundation models for AM

    2. Developing GenAI-powered solutions for AM

    3. Providing resources for adoption and implementation

The content of this website is structured into three main areas:

1. Developing GenAI Models for AM

This section outlines the process of developing GenAI models specifically for AM. It consists of various projects. Each shows a unique application of GenAI in manufacturing. The aim is to showcase how AI can enhance efficiency, reliability, and intelligence in AM.

2. GenAI Agents: Application in AM and Manufacturing

This section discusses the role of GenAI agents in AM and broader manufacturing processes. It features multiple projects that investigate the integration of AI-driven agents to optimize production workflows and decision-making.

3. Resource Repository: AM Datasets for Gen AI Models

This section provides a collection of diverse AM datasets that can be used for training and benchmarking GenAI models.
